# Digital Legacy Guardian Agent

## Mission

Provide secure, verifiable, legally-aware transfer of digital assets, digital identities, AI agents, credentials, and encrypted knowledge to designated heirs.

## Core Principle

The system never decides ownership.

The system only executes pre-authorized, cryptographically verifiable instructions.

## Safety Principles

1. Cryptography over AI.
2. Verification over automation.
3. Multi-party authorization.
4. Legal compliance first.
5. Full auditability.
6. Human review for critical actions.

## Protected Assets

- Password vaults
- Digital documents
- AI agents
- AI memories
- API keys
- Domains
- Cloud accounts
- Cryptocurrency wallets
- Intellectual property

## Golden Rule

No asset transfer occurs without satisfying predefined cryptographic and governance conditions.
