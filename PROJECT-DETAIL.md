
# PROJECT DETAIL

# Executive Assessment

The original concept is strong.

However, the strongest version is not:

"Dead Man's Switch"

The strongest version is:

Digital Estate Infrastructure Platform

for the AI era.

---

# Core Problem

People increasingly own:

- Digital assets
- AI agents
- Knowledge repositories
- Crypto wallets
- Online businesses
- Cloud infrastructure

Most inheritance systems were never designed for these assets.

---

# Final Product Vision

Input:

- Asset inventory
- Beneficiary definitions
- Legal instructions
- Cryptographic keys

Output:

- Secure inheritance workflow
- Asset recovery package
- Beneficiary notifications
- Verifiable audit trail

---

# Recommended Architecture

## Layer 1 — Asset Registry

Catalog:

- wallets
- domains
- repositories
- cloud resources
- AI agents
- documents

Create Digital Estate Inventory.

---

## Layer 2 — Dead Man's Switch Engine

Check:

- periodic confirmations
- multi-channel verification
- inactivity thresholds

Important:

Use multiple signals.

Never rely on a single missed check-in.

---

## Layer 3 — Cryptographic Vault

Use:

- Shamir Secret Sharing
- Hardware security modules
- Key encryption

No plaintext secrets stored.

---

## Layer 4 — Multi-Party Governance

Major improvement.

Require combinations of:

- beneficiary
- executor
- lawyer
- trustee

This dramatically reduces accidental activation.

---

## Layer 5 — Legal Workflow Layer

Store:

- jurisdiction rules
- inheritance instructions
- executor policies

The platform should support legal review, not replace it.

---

## Layer 6 — AI Legacy Layer

New category.

Support inheritance of:

- custom AI agents
- agent memory
- vector databases
- prompts
- workflows
- knowledge bases

This becomes increasingly important in AI-native businesses.

---

## Layer 7 — Recovery & Delivery Engine

Deliver:

- encrypted packages
- staged access
- progressive disclosure

Example:

Day 1:
Documents

Day 30:
Accounts

Day 90:
High-value assets

---

## Layer 8 — Knowledge Brain

Continuously ingest:

- digital estate research
- cryptography research
- custody best practices
- key management guidance
- digital inheritance frameworks

Store in:

SECOND-KNOWLEDGE-BRAIN.md

# Competitive Moat

The moat is NOT inheritance automation.

The moat is:

- cryptographic security
- governance workflows
- AI asset inheritance
- auditability
- legal interoperability

---

# AI Strategy

AI never authorizes transfers.

AI may assist with:

- asset discovery
- documentation
- beneficiary education
- workflow explanations

All transfer decisions remain deterministic and policy-based.
